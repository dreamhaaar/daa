About gulDe

In the Philippines, around 1.93 million people were unemployed as of March 2025. A key barrier to employment is the complex and often overwhelming process of gathering required documents—like NBI clearances, medical certificates, and valid IDs. Without proper documentation, job seekers face delays in hiring, payroll setup, and access to social security benefits.

For many first-time and low-income job seekers, this process is stressful due to a lack of guidance, time, and financial resources. Research shows that digital solutions, particularly web-based tracking systems, can significantly improve efficiency and user experience.

Our platform offers an accessible, tech-driven solution to simplify document collection, using smart task prioritization to reduce delays and support job seekers in navigating this critical step toward employment.

Developed by PUP students, this web app addresses the challenges faced by first-time job seekers in the Philippines by streamlining the document acquisition process. Using a topological sort approach, the app prioritizes and tracks essential employment documents, offering a user-friendly solution to reduce delays and simplify the path to employment.
